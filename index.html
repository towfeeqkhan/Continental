Top Places

Srinagar

Srinagar is the capital city of Jammu & Kashmir ideally located along the banks of the Jhelum River, Surrounded by the Himalayas, the city boasts amazing landscapes, sheer natural beauty, mesmerizing gardens, Shikara ride, houseboats, and much more.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a snow paradise, a cup-shaped valley ideally nestled in the Pir Panjal range of the western Himalayas. One of the best skiing destinations in India, From sheer natural beauty to snow adventure, this valley has a lot to offer to every kind of tourist.

Sonmarg

Famous as the ‘Meadow of Golden Light’, Sonamarg is a popular tourist destination in Kashmir. It is derived from two Hindi words, ‘Sona’ meaning gold and ‘Marg’ meaning meadow. For adventure lovers, it offers activities such as trekking, skiing, hiking, fishing, river rafting, etc

Gurez Valley

If Kashmir is the paradisiacal crown of India, then Gurez Valley is the most precious jewel in that crown. Forbidden for many years owing to its proximity to the Line of Control, the remote valley has now opened up to emerge as one of the most safe and peaceful places in the entire Kashmir valley. For many, this untouched region is perhaps the most beautiful place in Kashmir.

Bungus Valley

Bungus Valley provides an unforgettable experience for those seeking solitude in nature, thrilling adventures, or immersion in Kashmir’s rich cultural traditions. So pack your bags, venture into this hidden gem of Kashmir, and create memories that will last a lifetime in the lap of nature’s splendour.
